Netball		 Make a series of passes to team mates moving towards a scoring area. Use a chest pass and shoulder pass to support team in scoring. Use all three passes (chest, shoulder & bounce) correctly. Know which pass is best to use and when in a game.
Show some signs of using a chest pass and shoulder pass. Make decisions regarding which is the best type of pass to use. Use a range of speeds within a game to support a team in scoring. Use a range of square & straight passes to change direction of the ball.
Show a target to indicate where I'd like to pass to. Begin to use a bounce pass, which only bounces once. Begin to use square (across the court) & straight (up & down the court) passes to achieve pace. Use landing foot to change direction to lose a defender.
